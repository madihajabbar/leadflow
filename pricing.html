Questions about pricing
Quick FAQ
  • Is LeadFlow really free to use?
    Yes — the tools (lead generator, email writer, outreach builder) are free to use while LeadFlow is in beta. You can explore, test, and screenshot flows without paying.
  • What do the paid packages actually include?
    Paid packages are service offers — not software. You’re hiring the studio to take what LeadFlow generates and turn it into a real, usable system inside your tools (Sheets, CRMs, email sender, etc.).
  • Do you work with small businesses or only tech companies?
    Both. LeadFlow is designed for agencies, online coaches, local service businesses, and SaaS founders who want more structured outbound without building a huge team.
  • I’m not ready for a full build. Where should I start?
    Start with the sandbox plan. Use the tools to clarify your niche, offer, and messaging. When you’re ready, you can book an Automation Setup project and ship a lean version of your outbound system.
  • Can pricing be customized for my situation?
    Yes. The numbers above are starting points. If you have multiple brands, complex funnels, or very specific constraints, you can discuss a custom scope on a call.
